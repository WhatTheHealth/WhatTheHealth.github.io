Food

Diet plays a pivotal role in supplying your body with the nutrients it needs to think, move, and recover optimally. We compiled cutting edge research on specific diets and allergens to show what foods and diets can be most effective in increasing your energy, mental clarity, and quality of life.

Activity

Staying active is a crucial component in increasing your energy levels and happiness. Research shows exercise and time outdoors are crucial to our health and happiness. We compiled research on the best kinds of exercise, taking into account your level of fitness and your specific goals.

Mindfulness

Mindfulness exercises continue to impress researchers with its ability to fundamentally rewire how we relate to ourselves, other humans, and the world at large. There are many ways to be mindful -- we outline options, instructions, and plans so you can stay engaged and reap the maximum benefits.
